What is an E-Learning Platform?

An e-learning platform is a digital space that allows users to access educational content and courses online. It’s used by schools, universities, businesses, and individuals for learning and training purposes — anytime, anywhere.

Key Features of E-Learning Platforms:

  • Online Courses – Pre-recorded or live video lessons on various subjects
  • Quizzes & Assignments – Tools to test understanding and track progress.
  • Certificates – Completion badges or certificates to show your achievements
  • Interactive Content – Videos, animations, simulations, and gamified learning
  • Discussion Forums – Spaces for learners to ask questions and connect.
  • Mobile Access – Learn on the go with responsive design or mobile apps.
