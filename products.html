Top Products of E-Learning

1.Online Courses
  • Video-based or interactive lessons.
  • Can be self-paced or instructor-led
  • Subjects range from math and science to coding and business

2.Learning Management Systems (LMS)
  • Platforms like Moodle, Canvas, or Google Classroom.
  • Allow educators to manage content, assignments, and student progress

3. Certifications & Badges
  • Proof of course completion or skill mastery.
  • Useful for career growth (e.g., Coursera, edX).

4. E-Books & Digital Study Materials
  • Downloadable resources like PDFs, presentations, or interactive books.
